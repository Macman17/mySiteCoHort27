Where It All Began

Military Life

I had no idea what to expect when I joined the military, but living with my mother prepared me for the stressors of the military. My first job in the military was a supply warehouse specialist. When I reenlisted I decided to change jobs in order to gain more skills so I decided to become a career retention specialist. This allowed me face some of my fears and took me out of my confort zone, which I loved. I got out of the military in 2017 in hopes of starting my own business and finishing school.

School
